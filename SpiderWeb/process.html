OUR PROCESS

At Spider-Web, the customer's convenience is the priority. Our sign up process is as simple as it gets.

Contact Us

Send us a request using this page.

Feel free to include as little or as much information about your preferences as you like. We have a talented team of designers that can help you make decisions.

Discovery Phase

Our team will schedule an interview with you within a week.

In this phase, we research your target audience, and come up with specific goals for the project. We want our website to be a perfect fit for your needs. We will take into account your branding and design a website that feels right.

Planning

We draft an outline to use as a starting point.

Before starting to build your website, we work on the content that will fill it. We also decide on the site layout, and we create an SEO strategy.

Design

We continue to work on the visual look of your website.

We will decide on a color palette, typography, and any images you will use.

Development

We take over from here.

Our team will send you weekly updates on our progress. Depending on the complexity of your project, this phase can be completed in 2-5 weeks.

Launch

Your website is ready!

We will take care of publishing your website, all you need to do is click on it.

Maintenance

Keep in touch.

We will update your page regularly to make sure it meets modern standards. Let us know if you want us to update or change anything. With our Platinum plan, you can contact us any time.
